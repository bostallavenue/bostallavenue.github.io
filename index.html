About Us

Bostall Avenue Limited is a commodity trading company registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA). We operate as a general trading company with special focus on a variety of agro-allied products including unrefined organic shea butter, raw cashew nuts, hibiscus flower, sesame seed and many more. The company is managed by young, vibrant and dedicated professionals who understand local and international trading business dynamics based on their vast knowledge and experiences.

We adopt a blend of traditional and cutting edge trading techniques to ensure that your trading experiences with us are second to none. Our team of experts work within the primary local market where the products are harvested, packaged and transported according to your requests. We also have a team of experts in the UK from where sales of our products will be sold on Amazon.
